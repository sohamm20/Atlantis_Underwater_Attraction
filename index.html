Atlantis Underwater Attraction

About Atlantis

Atlantis is a legendary city that was said to have sunk beneath the ocean waves thousands of years ago. The underwater attraction is located at the site where Atlantis is believed to have been located, and offers visitors a chance to explore the ruins and see the marine life that now inhabits the area.

The attraction is staffed by a team of marine biologists and divers who work to maintain the safety of visitors and the health of the marine ecosystem.

Location

The Atlantis Underwater Attraction is located off the coast of Bermuda, and can be accessed via boat or submarine. The nearest port is Hamilton, Bermuda, and there are several hotels and resorts in the area that offer shuttle services to the attraction.
